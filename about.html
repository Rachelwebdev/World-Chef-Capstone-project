WORLD CHEFS GLOBAL COMPETITION 2023

World Chefs Congress brings together chef delegates and professionals from across the five continents to network and expand their professional bonds. The Global Chefs Challenge features top chefs from across the globe. These chefs must compete to show their skills in the kitchen and their adaptability under fire. Their culinary creations must follow guidelines such as being appetizing and tasteful, while also showing off exemplary plating for ease of service.
